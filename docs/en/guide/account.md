# Account, Settings & Billing

## Settings

**Settings** (`/settings`) holds your profile and preferences.

![Account settings](/images/console/settings.png)

## Billing

**Billing** (`/billing`) shows your credits balance and plan. Certain AI
operations consume credits; the balance is shown here.

![Billing and plan](/images/console/billing.png)

## OAuth login

If configured by an admin, you can sign in with Google. On first OAuth sign-in
you complete a short account-binding step that links the external identity to
your Devify account.

## Admin: management console

Administrators configure runtime settings from the management console. For
self-hosted instances, see the configuration guides:

- [LLM providers](/en/self-hosting/configuration/llm-providers.md)
- [App settings & model binding](/en/self-hosting/configuration/app-settings.md)
- [Email & notifications](/en/self-hosting/configuration/email-notifications.md)
