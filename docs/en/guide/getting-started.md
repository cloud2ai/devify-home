## Welcome to AImyChats

AImyChats is an AI-powered chat history assistant that helps you save, organize, and understand your conversations with image recognition and multi-scenario analysis.

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit the AImyChats platform (aimychats.com) and sign up for a free account. Once registered, you'll receive a unique email address for forwarding conversations.

### 2. Get Your Unique Email Address

After logging in, navigate to Settings to find your dedicated forwarding email address:

```
your-unique-id@aimychats.com
```

### 3. Forward Conversations

To save a conversation:

1. Open the conversation you want to save
2. Forward it to your AImyChats email address
3. AImyChats will automatically process and organize it
4. Access your saved conversation in the dashboard

## Core Concepts

### Conversations

AImyChats treats each forwarded email or message thread as a conversation. Conversations are automatically:

- Parsed and structured
- Summarized by AI
- Categorized by context
- Made searchable

### AI Image Recognition + Multi-Scenario Analysis

Our core advantages - AI recognizes images and analyzes based on scenarios:

**Image Recognition**:
- **Auto-recognize image content**: Screenshots, charts, photos
- **OCR text extraction**: Extract text from images
- **Chart data understanding**: Analyze data charts and statistics

**Multi-Scenario Smart Analysis**:
- **Select scenario**: Work, Customer Support, Product Discussion, Technical Support, etc.
- **Targeted analysis**: Different scenarios, different focus points
- **Support scenario**: Extract customer issues and requests
- **Product scenario**: Identify feature requirements and details
- **Work scenario**: Extract action items and assignees

**Text+Image+Scenario Combined**:
- **Topic Identification**: Accurately identify topics using text, images, AND scenario context
- **Key Information Extraction**: Extract scenario-relevant important info from text AND images
- **More Accurate Summaries**: Because AI "understands" images AND adapts to scenario needs!

This is what makes AImyChats different - we don't just save and understand content, we understand the context.

### Storage & Retention

Different plans offer different email quotas, storage, and retention periods:

- **Free**: 5 emails/month, 1 GB storage, 30-day retention
- **Starter**: 100 emails/month, 5 GB storage, 1-year retention
- **Standard**: 500 emails/month, 10 GB storage, 3-year retention
- **Pro**: 2000 emails/month, 20 GB storage, permanent retention
