## General

### What is AImyChats?

AImyChats is an AI-powered conversation management platform that helps you centralize, organize, and understand communications from email, WhatsApp, and other channels.

### How does AImyChats work?

1. Forward conversations to your unique AImyChats email address
2. Our system automatically processes the content
3. AI analyzes and generates summaries
4. Attachments are stored in the cloud
5. Access everything through our searchable dashboard

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously:
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Storage uses AES-256 encryption
- Only you can access your data
- Regular security audits and backups

## Using Devify

### How do I save conversations?

Simply forward emails or messages to your unique AImyChats email address. The system will automatically process and organize them.

### What types of content are supported?

- Text messages
- Images (permanently stored)
- File attachments
- Voice messages (transcription coming soon)
- Videos (coming soon)

### How accurate are AI summaries?

Our AI models achieve over 90% accuracy. However, accuracy may vary with:
- Very short conversations
- Heavy use of jargon or technical terms
- Mixed languages

You can always edit AI-generated summaries.

### How do I search my conversations?

Use the search bar to find conversations. Supports:
- Full-text search
- Date range filtering
- Category filtering
- Keyword highlighting

### Can I share conversations with others?

Direct sharing isn't currently supported, but you can:
- Export as PDF and share
- Use API integration for custom solutions
- Contact us about enterprise collaboration features

## Technical Issues

### I didn't receive a confirmation email

Possible reasons:
- Email in spam folder
- Incorrect email address
- Email server delay

Solution: Check spam folder or click "Resend".

### Forwarded emails aren't being processed

Check:
- Using correct unique email address
- Email size within plan limits
- Supported attachment formats

If issues persist, contact support with the send timestamp.

### Images not displaying

Possible causes:
- Image still uploading (please wait)
- Unsupported image format
- Storage quota exceeded

### How do I export my data?

Data export feature is coming soon! Stay tuned.
